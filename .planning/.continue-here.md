# BLOCKING CONSTRAINTS - Read Before Anything Else

> These are not suggestions. Each constraint below was discovered through failure.
> Acknowledge each one explicitly before proceeding.

- [ ] CONSTRAINT: Fresh-process live smoke is the gate. Same-process runs hid cold-start failures in `smoke-industry-01`, `smoke-industry-02`, and `smoke-mixed-02`. Always validate with `WASC_RETRIEVAL_MODE=live`, `WASC_LIVE_FIXTURE_SHORTCUTS_ENABLED=0`, `--smoke-gate` or `skill.benchmark.worker`, and a unique `WASC_LIVE_CACHE_DIR`.
- [ ] CONSTRAINT: Do not reintroduce thin RSS-as-evidence behavior. `google_news_rss` can help discovery, but the failure mode was "good title, thin body, no publisher grounding." Keep publisher resolution and fetch in the loop before treating news as canonical evidence.
- [ ] CONSTRAINT: Do not optimize to local smoke only. Earlier mixed/pooling tweaks improved local-looking numbers without fixing hidden-like generalization. Retain only structural changes that survive hidden-style fresh-process validation.
- [ ] CONSTRAINT: Do not make `mixed` a heavier first-hop route without artifact-backed evidence. The latest structural mixed experiment (`generated-hidden-like-mixed-r1-v3`) stayed `0 / 10` and regressed cases that previously at least returned `insufficient_evidence`.

## Critical Anti-Patterns

| Pattern | Description | Severity | Prevention Mechanism |
|---------|-------------|----------|----------------------|
| Same-process optimism | Warm shared-client runs hid the real cold-start failure modes. | blocking | Re-run with fresh workers and a new `WASC_LIVE_CACHE_DIR` before trusting any improvement. |
| Thin Google News evidence | RSS-only title/source/date hits looked plausible but were too thin to ground answers. | advisory | Resolve toward publisher URLs and fetch text before canonicalizing. |
| Empty-artifact optimism | Empty or partial benchmark directories looked like evidence but were not artifact-backed. | advisory | Only treat directories with `benchmark-summary.json` and `benchmark-runs.jsonl` as durable baselines. |
| Mixed-first-hop inflation | Promoting more impact/effect queries into first-hop `mixed` increased timeout pressure and did not improve mixed success. | blocking | Keep primary route stable and only consider supplemental evidence through cheaper post-primary or answer-time paths. |
| Intent marker sprawl | Expanding business-term marker lists looked plausible but was not maintainable and still failed mixed generalization. | advisory | Keep normalization/tokenization utilities, but stop using business vocabulary enumeration as the main routing strategy. |
